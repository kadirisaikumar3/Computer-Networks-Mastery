# Common Mistakes

---

# Mistake 1

## Thinking Firewalls Stop Every Attack

❌ Wrong

Firewall blocks every cyber attack.

✅ Correct

Firewalls significantly improve security but cannot stop every attack.

---

# Mistake 2

## Confusing Firewall and Antivirus

❌ Wrong

Firewall and Antivirus are the same.

✅ Correct

Firewall filters network traffic.

Antivirus scans files and removes malware.

---

# Mistake 3

## Thinking Packet Filtering is Stateful

❌ Wrong

Packet Filtering tracks sessions.

✅ Correct

Only Stateful Firewalls track active connections.

---

# Mistake 4

## Thinking Proxy Firewall Provides Direct Communication

❌ Wrong

Client directly contacts server.

✅ Correct

Proxy Firewall acts as an intermediary.

---

# Mistake 5

## Ignoring Rule Order

❌ Wrong

Rule order doesn't matter.

✅ Correct

The first matching rule is applied.

---

# Mistake 6

## Thinking NGFW Only Filters Packets

❌ Wrong

NGFW only checks headers.

✅ Correct

NGFW performs Deep Packet Inspection and application-aware filtering.

---

# Mistake 7

## Allowing All Ports

Only required ports should be allowed.

Follow the Principle of Least Privilege.

---

# Interview Tip

Inspect

Rules
