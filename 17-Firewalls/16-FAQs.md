# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. What is a Firewall?

A security device or software that filters network traffic.

---

## 2. Why is a Firewall needed?

To protect systems from unauthorized access.

---

## 3. What are the common Firewall types?

- Packet Filtering
- Stateful
- Proxy
- NGFW

---

## 4. Which Firewall tracks active sessions?

Stateful Firewall.

---

## 5. What is DPI?

Deep Packet Inspection.

---

## 6. Which Firewall acts as an intermediary?

Proxy Firewall.

---

## 7. Which Firewall provides the highest security?

Next-Generation Firewall.

---

## 8. What is a Firewall Rule?

A policy that allows or blocks traffic.

---

## 9. Does a Firewall replace Antivirus?

No.

Both serve different purposes.

---

## 10. Why is rule order important?

The first matching rule is applied.
