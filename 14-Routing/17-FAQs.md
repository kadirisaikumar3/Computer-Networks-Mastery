# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. What is Routing?

Routing is the process of forwarding packets between networks.

---

## 2. Which device performs Routing?

Router.

---

## 3. Which OSI layer performs Routing?

Network Layer (Layer 3).

---

## 4. What is a Routing Table?

A table that stores routes used to forward packets.

---

## 5. What is Static Routing?

Routes are manually configured.

---

## 6. What is Dynamic Routing?

Routes are automatically learned using routing protocols.

---

## 7. What is RIP?

A Distance Vector Routing Protocol.

---

## 8. What metric does RIP use?

Hop Count.

---

## 9. What is OSPF?

A Link-State Routing Protocol.

---

## 10. What metric does OSPF use?

Cost.

---

## 11. What is BGP?

A Path Vector Routing Protocol used on the Internet.

---

## 12. What is Longest Prefix Match?

The router selects the most specific matching route.

---
