# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive learning environment for everyone.

Everyone is encouraged to participate regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

---

# 🤝 Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and professional
- Helping others learn
- Giving constructive feedback
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

---

# 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive language
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting others
- Spam or self-promotion
- Sharing misleading or harmful content

---

# 💬 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Open a GitHub Issue (if appropriate)
- Contact the repository owner
- Provide clear details so the issue can be reviewed

All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

# ⚖️ Enforcement

Contributors who violate this Code of Conduct may have their comments, issues, or pull requests removed.

Repeated violations may result in being blocked from contributing.

---

# ❤️ Our Goal

Our goal is to make **Computer Networks Mastery** a friendly, helpful, and professional learning resource for everyone.

Thank you for helping build a positive community! 🚀
