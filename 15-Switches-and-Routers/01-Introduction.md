# Introduction

## What are Switches and Routers?

Switches and Routers are two of the most important networking devices.

Although they look similar, they perform different tasks.

- **Switch** connects devices within the same network (LAN).
- **Router** connects different networks together.

Together, they enable communication across homes, offices, campuses, and the Internet.

---

# Why Do We Need Them?

Without Switches:

- Computers in the same office cannot communicate.

Without Routers:

- Different networks cannot communicate.
- Internet access is not possible.

---

The Switch connects local devices.

# Main Difference

Switch

↓

Connects devices in the same network.

Router

↓

Connects different networks.

---

# OSI Layer

Switch

- Layer 2 (Data Link Layer)

Router

- Layer 3 (Network Layer)

# Real-Life Analogy

Imagine a college.
