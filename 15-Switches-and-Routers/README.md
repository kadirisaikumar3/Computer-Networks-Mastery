# 🌐 Chapter 15 – Switches & Routers

## 📖 Overview

**Switches** and **Routers** are two of the most important networking devices.

- A **Switch** connects devices within the same Local Area Network (LAN).
- A **Router** connects different networks and forwards packets between them.

Together, they form the backbone of modern computer networks.
