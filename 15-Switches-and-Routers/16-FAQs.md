# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. What is a Switch?

A Layer 2 device that connects devices in a LAN.

---

## 2. What is a Router?

A Layer 3 device that connects different networks.

---

## 3. Which address does a Switch use?

MAC Address.

---

## 4. Which address does a Router use?

IP Address.

---

## 5. What is a MAC Address Table?

A table that maps MAC addresses to switch ports.

---

## 6. What is a Routing Table?

A table that stores routes for packet forwarding.

---

## 7. What is a VLAN?

A logical network within a switch.

---

## 8. Which device separates Broadcast Domains?

Router.

---

## 9. Which device creates separate Collision Domains?

Switch.

---

## 10. Which switching technique performs CRC checking?

Store-and-Forward.

---
