# Why TCP/IP?

## Why Do We Need the TCP/IP Model?

The TCP/IP Model provides a universal framework that enables computers around the world to communicate over interconnected networks.

---

# Problems Solved

Without TCP/IP:

- Different systems couldn't communicate easily.
- Internet communication would not be standardized.
- Routing between networks would be difficult.

---

# Benefits

## Standard Communication

All Internet-connected devices follow TCP/IP rules.

---

## Interoperability

Allows communication between devices running different operating systems.

---

## Scalability

Supports billions of devices connected to the Internet.

---

## Reliability

TCP ensures reliable delivery of data.

---

## Flexibility

Works across different hardware and network technologies.
