# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. What is Network Security?

Protecting networks and data from cyber threats.

---

## 2. What is the CIA Triad?

Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability.

---

## 3. What is Authentication?

Verifying identity.

---

## 4. What is Authorization?

Granting permissions after authentication.

---

## 5. What is Encryption?

Converting plaintext into ciphertext.

---

## 6. What is Hashing?

Generating a fixed-size one-way hash.

---

## 7. Which encryption is faster?

Symmetric Encryption (AES).

---

## 8. Which encryption uses two keys?

Asymmetric Encryption (RSA/ECC).

---

## 9. What is TLS?

A protocol for secure communication.

---

## 10. Why is VPN used?

To create a secure encrypted connection over the Internet.
