# Interview Tips

## 1. Understand, Don't Memorize

Interviewers ask "Why?" more often than "What?".

---

## 2. Draw Diagrams

Explain packet flow visually.

Example:

```
Client

↓

DNS

↓

Server

↓

Response
```

---

## 3. Give Real-World Examples

Examples:

- Browser Opening Google
- Online Banking
- Video Streaming
- WhatsApp Calls

---

## 4. Compare Similar Concepts

- TCP vs UDP
- HTTP vs HTTPS
- Switch vs Router
- Encryption vs Hashing
- Authentication vs Authorization

---

## 5. Learn Port Numbers

Know common ports by heart.

---

## 6. Practice Commands

- ping
- tracert
- ipconfig
- nslookup
- netstat
- arp
- route

---

## 7. Practice Whiteboard Explanations

Explain networking concepts without notes.

---

## 8. Speak Clearly

Structure answers as:

Definition

↓

Working

↓

Example

↓

Use Case

---

# Golden Rule

Don't just define a concept.

Explain:

- Why?
- How?
- Where?
