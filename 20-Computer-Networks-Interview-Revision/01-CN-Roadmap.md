# Complete Computer Networks Roadmap

## Why Learn Computer Networks?

Computer Networks is one of the most important subjects for Software Development Engineer (SDE) interviews. It helps you understand how computers communicate, how the Internet works, and how distributed applications exchange data.

Top companies like **Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Oracle, Cisco, Adobe, Walmart, and Atlassian** frequently ask Computer Networks questions in technical interviews.

# Google Interview Preparation Tips

- Understand concepts instead of memorizing definitions.
- Learn packet flow between client and server.
- Practice comparing similar technologies.
- Know real-world use cases.
- Revise networking commands.
- Practice scenario-based interview questions.
