# Common Mistakes

---

# Mistake 1

Thinking the Internet is the only network.

Correct:

The Internet is the largest WAN.

---

# Mistake 2

Confusing Switch and Router.

Switch:

Works inside a LAN using MAC addresses.

Router:

Connects different networks using IP addresses.

---

# Mistake 3

Confusing Hub and Switch.

Hub broadcasts data.

Switch forwards data intelligently.

---

# Mistake 4

Thinking all topologies are equally reliable.

Correct:

Mesh is the most reliable.

---

# Mistake 5

Thinking Client-Server and Peer-to-Peer are the same.

Client-Server has a dedicated server.

Peer-to-Peer does not.
