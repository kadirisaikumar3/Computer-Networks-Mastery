# Google Interview Corner

This section contains the most commonly asked introduction-level Computer Networks interview questions.

---

# Basic Questions

## What is a Computer Network?

A group of interconnected devices that communicate and share resources.

---

## Why do we need Computer Networks?

To enable communication, resource sharing, and Internet connectivity.

---

## What is a protocol?

A set of rules that governs communication between devices.

---

## What are the components of a Computer Network?

- Computers
- Switches
- Routers
- Cables
- NIC
- Protocols

---

## Difference between LAN and WAN.

| LAN | WAN |
|-----|-----|
| Small Area | Large Area |
| Faster | Slower |
| Low Cost | High Cost |

---

## Difference between Hub and Switch.

| Hub | Switch |
|------|---------|
| Broadcasts Data | Uses MAC Address |
| Layer 1 | Layer 2 |

---

## Difference between Switch and Router.

| Switch | Router |
|----------|---------|
| Uses MAC | Uses IP |
| Layer 2 | Layer 3 |

---

## Difference between Client-Server and Peer-to-Peer.

| Client-Server | Peer-to-Peer |
|---------------|--------------|
| Dedicated Server | No Dedicated Server |
| High Security | Low Security |

---

# Scenario-Based Questions

## Which topology is used in offices?

Star Topology.

---

## Which topology offers the highest reliability?

Mesh Topology.

---

## Which network covers an entire city?

MAN.

---

## Which network covers the world?

WAN.
