# Types of Computer Networks

Computer Networks are classified based on geographical coverage.

---

# PAN (Personal Area Network)

Coverage:

```
1–10 meters
```

Examples:

- Bluetooth
- Smart Watch
- Wireless Earbuds

---

# LAN (Local Area Network)

Coverage:

Single Building

Examples:

- Home Wi-Fi
- Office Network
- College Lab

Advantages:

- High Speed
- Low Cost

---

# MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

Coverage:

Entire City

Examples:

- City-wide Internet
- Cable TV Networks

---

# WAN (Wide Area Network)

Coverage:

Countries or Continents

Examples:

- Internet
- Banking Network
- Airline Reservation System

---

# Comparison

| Network | Coverage | Example |
|----------|----------|----------|
| PAN | Few meters | Bluetooth |
| LAN | Building | Office |
| MAN | City | Cable TV |
| WAN | Worldwide | Internet |

---

# Which Network is the Internet?

The Internet is the world's largest:

```
WAN
```

# Google Interview Questions

## Difference between LAN and WAN?

LAN covers a small area.

WAN covers large geographical regions.

---

## Which network is fastest?

LAN.

---

## Which network has the largest coverage?

WAN.
