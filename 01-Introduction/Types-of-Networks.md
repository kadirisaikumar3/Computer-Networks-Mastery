# Types of Computer Networks

A Computer Network can be classified based on its geographical coverage.

---

# 1. PAN (Personal Area Network)

A PAN is the smallest type of computer network.

It connects devices within a few meters of a person.

### Examples

- Bluetooth
- Smart Watch
- Wireless Keyboard
- Earbuds

### Range

Approximately 10 meters.

### Real World Example

```
Phone
     ↕
Bluetooth
     ↕
Smart Watch
```

### Advantages

- Low Cost
- Easy to Setup
- Low Power Consumption

### Disadvantages

- Very Short Range
- Low Speed

---

# 2. LAN (Local Area Network)

A LAN connects computers within a limited area.

Examples include:

- Home
- School
- College
- Office

### Range

Up to a few kilometers.

### Real World Example

```
Computer
     │
     │
 Switch
  │  │  │
 PC PC Printer
```

### Advantages

- High Speed
- Easy File Sharing
- Secure

### Disadvantages

- Limited Coverage

---

# 3. MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

A MAN connects multiple LANs across a city.

Examples:

- University Campuses
- City-wide Internet

### Range

10–100 km

### Real World Example

```
Office A
     │
     │
 Internet
     │
Office B
```

### Advantages

- Covers Entire City
- High Speed

### Disadvantages

- Expensive
- Complex Maintenance

---

# 4. WAN (Wide Area Network)

A WAN connects networks across countries and continents.

The Internet is the largest WAN.

### Examples

- Internet
- Bank Networks
- Google Data Centers

### Real World Example

```
India
   │
Internet
   │
USA
```

### Advantages

- Global Connectivity
- Resource Sharing

### Disadvantages

- High Cost
- Higher Latency

---

# Comparison

| Network | Range | Example |
|----------|-------|----------|
| PAN | 10 meters | Bluetooth |
| LAN | Building | Office |
| MAN | City | University |
| WAN | World | Internet |

---

# Interview Questions

### What is PAN?

A Personal Area Network used for connecting nearby personal devices.

---

### Difference between LAN and WAN?

LAN covers a small area and provides higher speed.

WAN covers large geographical areas but usually has higher latency.

---

### Which network is the Internet?

The Internet is a WAN.

---

✔ PAN → Personal Devices
