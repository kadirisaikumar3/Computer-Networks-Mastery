# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. What is UDP?

User Datagram Protocol.

---

## 2. Which layer does UDP work on?

Transport Layer.

---

## 3. Is UDP reliable?

No.

---

## 4. Is UDP connection-oriented?

No.

---

## 5. What is the UDP Header size?

8 Bytes.

---

## 6. Does UDP use ACK?

No.

---

## 7. Does UDP retransmit packets?

No.

---

## 8. Does UDP guarantee order?

No.

---

## 9. Which applications use UDP?

- DNS
- DHCP
- VoIP
- Gaming
- Streaming
- SNMP
- TFTP

---

## 10. Why is UDP faster than TCP?

Because it has:

- No Handshake
- No ACK
- No Retransmission
- Small Header
