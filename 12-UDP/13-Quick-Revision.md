## UDP in One Line

UDP (User Datagram Protocol) is a **connectionless**, **fast**, and **lightweight** Transport Layer protocol.

---

# UDP Characteristics

- Connectionless
- Fast
- Lightweight
- Low Latency
- No ACK
- No Retransmission
- No Flow Control
- No Congestion Control

---

# TCP vs UDP

| TCP | UDP |
|------|-----|
| Reliable | Unreliable |
| Connection-Oriented | Connectionless |
| ACK | No ACK |
| Retransmission | No Retransmission |
| Slower | Faster |
