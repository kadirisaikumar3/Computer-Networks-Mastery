# Common Mistakes in IP Addressing

These are common mistakes candidates make during interviews.

---

## Mistake 1

❌ IP Address and MAC Address are the same.

✅ Correct

IP Address identifies a device logically.

MAC Address identifies a device physically.

---

## Mistake 2

❌ IPv6 is faster than IPv4.

✅ Correct

IPv6 mainly solves the address exhaustion problem and improves scalability. Performance depends on many factors.

---

## Mistake 3

❌ Private IP Addresses work directly on the Internet.

✅ Correct

Private IP Addresses require NAT to communicate over the Internet.

---

## Mistake 4

❌ Static IP is always better.

✅ Correct

Static IPs are useful for servers, while Dynamic IPs are more practical for home and office devices.

---

## Mistake 5

❌ Every device on the Internet has a Public IP.

✅ Correct

Most devices use Private IP Addresses behind a router that performs NAT.

---

# Interview Advice

Always explain concepts with a simple real-world example. Interviewers value understanding over memorized definitions.
