# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## What is an IP Address?

A unique logical address assigned to a device.

---

## How many bits are in IPv4?

32 Bits.

---

## How many bits are in IPv6?

128 Bits.

---

## What is the purpose of a subnet mask?

To separate the Network ID from the Host ID.

---

## What is CIDR?

A classless IP addressing method using prefix notation.

---

## Why was IPv6 introduced?

To overcome IPv4 address exhaustion.

---

## What is NAT?

Network Address Translation.

---

## What is the Loopback Address?

127.0.0.1

---

## What is APIPA?

Automatic Private IP Addressing when DHCP fails.

---

## Which address identifies a network?

Network Address.

---

## Which address sends packets to all hosts?

Broadcast Address.
